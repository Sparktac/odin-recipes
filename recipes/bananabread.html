Banana Bread

The best banana bread you'll ever have!

Why compromise the banana flavor? This banana bread is moist and delicious with loads of banana flavor! Friends and family love this recipe and say it's by far the best! It's also wonderful toasted!!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas
